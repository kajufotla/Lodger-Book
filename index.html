Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our secure tools.

Are my files uploaded to any server?
No. PDFExpert processes all files locally in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your data never leaves your device, ensuring 100% privacy and security.
Does PDFExpert work offline?
Yes! Once the webpage loads in your browser, you can disconnect from the internet and continue using all the tools without any interruption.
Is there any file size limit?
Since processing happens on your device, the file size limit depends entirely on your device's RAM and processing power. However, standard files up to 200MB work seamlessly on most modern devices.
